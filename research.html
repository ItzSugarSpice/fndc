FNDC

Research & Development

The Federal Nuclear Development Center conducts research, development, testing, and evaluation activities across multiple nuclear science disciplines.

Strategic Systems Division

Research supporting strategic deterrence modernization and advanced systems integration.

Advanced Warhead Development Division

Engineering, modeling, and modernization initiatives supporting national defense objectives.

Reactor Technologies Division

Research and development of advanced nuclear reactor systems and supporting technologies.

Nuclear Materials Sciences Division

Advanced materials research supporting scientific, engineering, and operational requirements.
