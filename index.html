Message From The Facility Director

Alexander Anthony

Facility Director

The Federal Nuclear Development Center carries a responsibility matched by few institutions in our nation’s scientific and strategic landscape. Every program we advance, every system we refine, and every discovery we pursue contributes directly to the security, stability, and technological leadership of the United States.

Our mandate is clear: to uphold the highest standards of scientific rigor, operational integrity, and strategic foresight. As we modernize critical capabilities and expand the frontiers of nuclear research, we do so with an unwavering commitment to excellence and national service.

The challenges before us are complex, but our mission is steadfast. Together, we will continue to strengthen the nation’s deterrence posture, accelerate innovation in nuclear science, and ensure that FNDC remains a cornerstone of American strength for generations to come.
