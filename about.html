About the Federal Nuclear Development Center

The Federal Nuclear Development Center (FNDC) serves as the nation's leading institution for nuclear science, strategic deterrence research, advanced reactor development, and national security innovation.

Mission

To advance nuclear science and engineering through research, development, testing, and technological innovation in support of national security objectives.

Core Values

  • Scientific Excellence
  • National Security
  • Integrity
  • Innovation
  • Safety
  • Accountability
